Welcome to Laura A. Ermert's page

lermert (at) uw (dot) edu



I am a seismologist interested in ambient seismic sources and the way they illuminate the subsurface to provide us nearly continuous information about Earth structure. For my current work as postdoctoral scholar at University of Washington, I utilize ambient noise to study the site response of sedimentary basins, which is an important hazard factor during earthquakes. This may also reveal how sedimentary basins, which underlie many towns and cities, respond to environmental changes.
