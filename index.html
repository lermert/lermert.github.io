Find my contact details here.



I am a seismologist interested in ambient seismic sources and the way they illuminate the subsurface to provide us nearly continuous information about Earth structure. Currently, I work on a Chaire de Professeur Junior position at ISTerre. My project aims to develop ambient noise-based phase velocity mapping by modeling ambient noise cross-correlations including source information. More information about the project will go online soon.
